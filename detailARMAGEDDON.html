Armageddon

AESPA


Release Date: 27 May 2024


‘Armageddon’s’ music video and concept displays the members of æspa encountering their ‘evil selves’ in another world. According to the music video’s director and the æspa members themselves on Spotify’s Youtube Channel — ‘Armageddon’ is the start of æspa’s second era, where they meet themselves in different worlds and universes in order to define themselves; the individualistic motto of their second era being “Only I Can Define Myself,“ which appears in the song’s lyrics as well; complementing the gritty yet controlled tone, delivering a more refined and intense experience. ‘Armageddon’s’ post-apocalyptic-esque music video and concept displays the members of æspa encountering their ‘evil selves’ in another world. It’s a hip-hop dance heavy track featuring a powerful synth bass and a blend of old-school and contemporary essence; and showcases the æspa members' unique and exceptional vocals in each line, ending the song off with a impactful outro.
