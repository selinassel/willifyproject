Do It Like That

TOMORROW X TOGETHER


Release Date: 11 July 2023


“Do It Like That” marks a sensational collaboration between South Korea’s TOMORROW X TOGETHER, a talented and dynamic boy band, and the renowned American pop band Jonas Brothers. This highly anticipated track, released on July 7, 2023, is accompanied by an exhilarating music video masterfully produced by Ryan Tedder, known as the lead vocalist and frontman of the internationally acclaimed pop rock band OneRepublic. The fast-paced dance-pop track describes the moment when someone falls in love, with a fun and catchy lyrics. The song combines the refreshing voices of TOMORROW X TOGETHER and the Jonas Brothers for an uplifting summer jam. The song appears on TOMORROW X TOGETHER’s third studio album, The Name Chapter: FREEFALL.
