Blue Hour

TOMORROW X TOGETHER


Release Date: 26 October 2020


The meaning of “Blue Hour” talks about watching the sun set at 5:53. TXT describes this period of the day as “orange magic” and “the gap between the two worlds,” saying they never want this moment to end. After hearing the song, you’ll find a new appreciation for this time, too, because TXT paints such a gorgeous image of nightfall.
