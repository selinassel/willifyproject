Plot Twist

TWS


Release Date: 22 January 2024


“첫 만남은 계획대로 되지 않아 (plot twist),” the title track from TWS’s debut mini-album, Sparkling Blue, is an electrifying hybrid-pop song that perfectly captures the excitement and uncertainty of a first meeting. With its powerful drums, exhilarating guitar riffs, and captivating synth sounds, the song begins with an intro that feels like stepping into a dream. The contrasting lyrics further enhance the song’s message, portraying the awakening from a dream-like state. TWS, a rookie boy group, aims to bring forth naturalness and honesty in their music, and it’s immediately evident in their debut title song. Through “첫 만남은 계획대로 되지 않아 (plot twist),” they candidly express the excitement they feel at this very moment. The thrill and exhilaration of a first encounter resonate with everyone, and TWS channels this excitement into their debut song.
