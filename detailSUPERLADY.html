Super Lady

(G)I-DLE


Release Date: 29 January 2024


“Super Lady” is the lead single from the second full-length album, 2, by (G)I-DLE. This empowering song is dedicated to all the superwomen in the world, celebrating their beauty and strength. With its bold lyrics, the song encourages everyone to face hardships and adversity head-on while emphasizing the importance of unity and collective progress. Their unwavering confidence leads to a dynamic performance directed by Kirsten Dodgen, who has worked with global artists including Justin Bieber, Rihanna, Ciara, CL, and more. To convey the song’s message, the music video referenced powerful women such as Taylor Swift and Cruella in MIYEON and YUQI’s scenes.
